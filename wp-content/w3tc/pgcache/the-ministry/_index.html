About Us

Once Life Matters Ministries works in conjunction with Marty Angelo Ministries and others in proclaiming and teaching the gospel of the kingdom of God to prisoners, substance abuse recovery program clients, and troubled celebrities. The ministry utilizes life-changing books, evangelistic outreaches, and follow-up resources.
